The Health Ministry has invited former nurses and other healthcare professionals currently in locum or in private practice to help deliver patient care in public hospitals and community care sectors. More than 1,100 applications were submitted on the first day of the launch of the SG Healthcare Corps. Cheryl Goh with more.
